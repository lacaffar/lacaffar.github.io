01 — Mission Profile

Engineer by training,
stargazer by default.

There's a reason the company I work for is named Vernova — verde (green) meets nova (a new star). That collision is more or less my whole personality: clean energy on one shoulder, the cosmos on the other.

At GE Vernova I get to work on the hardware that actually moves electrons across the planet. The dream is to take that systems thinking and point it upward — at SpaceX, where energy and aerospace stop being two interests and start being one job.

I like problems that are slightly too big, hardware that hums, and the quiet thrill of watching a graph behave. If it converts energy from one form to another, I want to understand it.
