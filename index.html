About

I am a mechanical engineering student at NC State (GPA 3.889) who cares about one thing above all: building reliable hardware for the systems that power humanity.

That has taken me from a fusion lab studying reactor wall stability, to leading NC State's Windpack wind turbine club, to engineering wind hardware at GE Vernova. Right now I am designing a cryogenic rotary union to prove out the extreme thermal and sealing work that flight hardware demands.

The goal is simple: contribute to SpaceX, on the engineering that makes life multiplanetary.
